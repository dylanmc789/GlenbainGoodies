By Brooklyn

### Ingredients

* 4 eggs
* 4 tbsp. oil
* 2 cups milk
* ¼ tsp. salt
* 2 cups flour
* 1 tsp. sugar

### Instructions

Beat eggs in a large bowl until frothy. Add the rest of the ingredients. Beat until smooth. Grease a pan and put enough batter to just cover bottom of the pan. Tip and swirl batter to cover the pan. Flip when it turns golden brown. Makes about 24 crepes.

# Crepe Sauce

### Ingredients

* ½ Cup White Sugar
* ¼ Cup Water (or Ginger ale)
* 1 Tbsp. Cornstarch (mixed with Sugar)
* 1 Cup Mixed Berries

### Instructions

Combined all of the ingredients in a saucepan and cook until thickened on medium heat stirring constantly.
