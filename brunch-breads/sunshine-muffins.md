### Ingredients

* 1 orange cut up
* ½ cup orange juice
* 1 egg
* ¼ cup oil
* 1 ½ cups flour
* 1 tsp. baking powder
* ¾ cup sugar
* 1 tsp. salt
* 1 tsp. baking soda
* ½ cup raisins
* ½ cup chopped nuts

### Instructions

Mix orange, orange juice, egg and oil in blender. Blend until smooth. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt,blend again.Add nuts and raisins blend just until mixture is mixed.Pour mixture into muffin tins and bake at 350 F for 15-20 minutes.
