### Ingredients

* 2 Cups Zucchini
* 4 Tsp. Baking Soda
* 1 ½ Cup White Sugar
* 4 Tsp. Baking Powder
* 5 Eggs
* 2 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
* ¾ Cup Vegetable Oil
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla
* 3 Cups Flour
* ½ Cup Butter milk or Sour Cream
* 1 Tsp. Salt
* 1 Cup Nuts

### Instructions

Cream oil, sugar, and eggs, add remaining ingredients mix well. Bake at 350 for 50 minutes in greased in two large or 10 mini loaf pans.
