By Betty Crocker

### Ingredients

* 1 lemon Cake mix
* 3 Eggs
* 1 ½ Cups Shredded Zucchini
* 1 Tbsp. vegetable Oil
* 1 Tbsp. Lemon Zest or 2Tbsp. Lemon Juice

### Instructions

Combine ingredients and mix well. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes. (Opt. you can add ½ Cup of ground Poppy Seeds).

# Glaze

### Ingredients

* 1 ½ Tbsp. lemon Juice
* ½ Cup White Sugar
* 1 Cup Icing Sugar

### Instructions

Mix well and pour onto warm lemon loafs.
