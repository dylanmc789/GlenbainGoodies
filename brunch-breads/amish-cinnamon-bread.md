### Ingredients

**Starter Schedule**
* Day 1 (receive starter) no refrigerate
* Day 2, 3, 4 (stir)
* Day 5 (add 1 cup milk/flour/sugar then mix well)
* Day 6, 7 (stir)
* Day 8, 9 (Do Nothing)
* Day 10 (put in mixing bowl + add 1 cup sugar/milk/flour)

Stir well. Put 1/3 cup dough in 3 small containers (give 2 away)  
(1 to start over)

**Add**
* ½ cup oil
* 4 eggs
* 2 cups flour
* 1 pkg vanilla pudding (instant)
* 2 tsp vanilla
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1 cup white sugar

**Cinnamon Sugar**
* 2 tsp cinnamon
* ¼ cup brown sugar

### Instructions

Mix well. In separate bowl mix 2 tsp cinnamon and ¼ cup brown sugar. Grease 2 loaf pans. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. ¼ amount of batter in each pan sprinkle with cinnamon/sugar. Cover with rest of batter and rest cinnamon/sugar. Stir to marble. Bake 1 hour.
