### Ingredients

1 ½ Cups Flour  
1 Tsp. Baking Soda  
1 Tsp. Baking Powder  
½ Tsp. Salt  
1 Tsp. Cinnamon  
2 Eggs  
1 Cup Wh. Sugar  
¾ Cup Oil  
1 ½ Cup Pure Pumpkin  
1 Cup Chocolate Chips  

### Instructions

Beat eggs and sugar well, then add oil and stir in pumpkin.  
Combine dry ingredients and add to wet mixture.  
Add chocolate chips.  
Fill muffin tins 2/3 full and bake for 20 minutes at 350 F.  
These muffins freeze well.
