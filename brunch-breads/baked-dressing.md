### Ingredients

* 2 Cups water (less if bread not dried)
* 1 Envelope chicken noodle soup  
  *OR* 1 Tbsp. chicken base
* ½ Cup butter
* 1 Onion, chopped  
  *(or 1 pkg. onion soup mix)*
* 1 ½ Cups celery, chopped
* 1 Tbsp. poultry seasoning
* 3 Quarts bread cubes  
  *(about 30 slices / 12 cups)*

### Instructions

Simmer water and soup for 5 minutes.  
Melt butter, then add onions, celery, and poultry seasoning. Cook until tender.  
Mix all ingredients together with the bread cubes.  
Pack into a greased bread pan.  
Bake at 350°F for about 30 minutes, until browned and heated through.

### Notes

**Secret:** Cut bread while frozen. Let it sit on a tea towel for 2 days to fully dry.
