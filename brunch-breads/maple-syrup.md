### Ingredients

* 3 cups Brown Sugar
* 1 tsp. Maple extract
* 1 cup water

### Instructions

In medium Saucepan combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil stirring frequently and remove from heat. Serve with pancakes, waffles, and French toast. Makes about 3 cups.
