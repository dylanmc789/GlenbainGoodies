### Ingredients

* 2 cups flour
* 4 tsp. baking powder
* 1 tbsp. sugar
* ½ tsp. salt
* ½ cup margarine
* 1 egg beaten
* 2/3 cup milk

### Instructions

Add margarine to flour mixture and cut in until course like meal. Add egg and milk. Knead dough until soft. Roll out until ¾ thick. Bake for 10-15 minutes at 400 F. Makes about 18.
