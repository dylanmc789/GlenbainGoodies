### Ingredients

* 1 ½ cups flour
* 2 tsp. baking powder
* ¾ cup milk
* ¾ tsp. salt

### Instructions

Mix dry ingredients together, add milk and mix until it starts to from a ball. Drop by spoon full onto stew or soup about 20 minutes before serving.
