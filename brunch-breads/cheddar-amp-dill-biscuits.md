### Ingredients

* 2 Cups Flour
* 4 Tsp. Baking Powder
* 1 Tbsp. Sugar
* 3/4 Tsp. Salt
* 1 Tsp. Dill
* 1 Cup Milk
* 1/4 Cup Margarine
* 1 Cup Cheddar cheese

### Instructions

Combine all dry ingredients in a large bowl and cut in margarine to make a coarse meal texture. Then add milk and cheese, mix until combine. Drop by the spoonful unto cookie sheets and Bake at 350 for 12-15 minutes. \* This recipe does not make a rollable dough, you have to use two spoons, a cookie, or ice cream scoop to form the biscuits.\*
