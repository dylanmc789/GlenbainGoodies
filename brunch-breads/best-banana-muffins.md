### Ingredients

* 1 egg lightly beaten
* ½ cup sugar
* 6 tbsp. oil
* 1 cup ripe bananas
* 1 ¼ tsp. vanilla
* ½ cup all bran
* 1 cup flour
* 1 tsp. baking powder
* 1 tsp. baking soda
* 1 tsp. salt
* ½ cup raisins or choc, chips

### Instructions

Mix egg, sugar, oil, banana, vanilla and all bran together, let stand for 5 minutes. Then add flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and raisins or choc. chips. Bake at 350° for 20-25 minutes. Makes about a dozen.
