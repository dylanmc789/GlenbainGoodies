### Ingredients

* ½ Cup Soy sauce
* ½ Tsp. Garlic Powder
* 1 Tsp. Dry Mustard
* 2 Doz. Wings cut in half
* 4 Tbsp. Br. Sugar

### Instructions

Baste wings in mixture. Until coated. Marinate for 2 hours in refrigerator in flat baking pan covered. Bake in same pan for 1 hour at 375°. Baste while cooking
