### Ingredients

To Blend:

* 1 bag avocados (4-6)
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp sriracha
* 1 tbs garlic
* 4-6 tbs lemon or lime juice

To Dice:

* 1/3 Red onion
* 1 tomato
* 1 bunch of cilantro

### Instructions

1. If using fresh garlic, paste the garlic with the salt.  
2. Either mash or blend the avocados, garlic, sriracha, salt and lemon juice.  
3. Dice the onion, tomato, and cilantro. Mix into preferred serving bowl.  
4. Add blended ingredients to diced ingredients, mix well.  
5. Preferably cover and chill in fridge for approx an hour to allow flavors to mingle.  
6. Serve  
7. Easy Nachos : Oven 350 F, shred cheese on chips, bake 7 minutes or until cheese has hints of golden brown
