### Ingredients

1 ½ Cups Honey  
6 Tbsp. Soya Sauce  
2 Tbsp. Minced Garlic  
2 Pounds Chicken Wings  

### Instructions

Heat honey, soya sauce, and garlic in a saucepan and bring to a boil.  
Place the wings in a 9 x 13” baking pan and pour the honey mixture over the chicken.  
Cover with foil and marinate in the refrigerator for a few hours.  
Bake covered at 375 F for 1 hour, turning the wings after ½ hour.  
Remove the foil cover and bake for 15 minutes.  
Take the wings out of the sauce and bake on a rack for 10 minutes.  
Turn the chicken wings and cook for another 10 minutes.  
They are ready to eat.
