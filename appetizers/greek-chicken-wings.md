### Ingredients

* ½ Cup Greek Seasoning
* 2 Tbsp. Soya Sauce
* ½ Cup Olive Oil
* ¼ Cup Water
* 1 Tsp. Seasoning Salt
* 1 Tsp. Balsamic Vinegar Or Apple Cider Vinegar

### Instructions

Make this an hour or more ahead of time that way it was time to soften the Greek Seasoning. This recipe makes enough for about 24 chicken wings.
