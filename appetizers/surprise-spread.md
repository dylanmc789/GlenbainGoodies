### Ingredients

* 1 Pkg. 8 oz. Cream Cheese
* ½ Cup Sour Cream
* ¼ Cup Mayonnaise
* 3 Green onion, Chopped
* 1 Green Pepper, Chopped
* 1 Tomato, Diced
* 1 Pint of your favorite Salsa
* 2 Cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

### Instructions

Mix first three ingredients together. Spread over “12” pizza pan. Add layers of salsa, cheese, green pepper, onion, tomatoes. Cover and chill until ready to eat. Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.
