### Ingredients

* 1 cup white sugar
* 2 eggs well beaten
* 2/3 cup white vinegar
* 4 tsp Dry Mustard

### Instructions

Mix eggs and sugar in saucepan 1st, then add white vinegar and dry mustard. Heat until it thickens, serve either hot or cold. Store in a glass jar in the refrigerator.
