### Ingredients

* 1 ½ cup Canola oil
* 2 Pk. Ranch dressing (6 tbsp. bulk powder mix)
* 4 tsp. (rounded) of dill weed

In an extra large steel bowl mix the following:

* 2 boxes of mini Ritz
* 1 small box Cheerio’s (620 g. Or ~ 12 cups)
* 1 bag Pretzels (450 g.)
* 2 bags goldfish
* 3 bags bugles
* 1 small box Crispex (3 L or 13 cups)

### Instructions

Stir well, the oil and seasonings in a glass measuring cup.(If you heat the oil in the microwave for about a minute that will help dissolve the ranch powder). Pour over the cereals and toss to coat. Let stand overnight. \*An extra large plastic bag also works well to mix cereals together.
