### Ingredients

* ¼ Cup Melted Butter
* 1 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
* ½ Tsp. Garlic
* ½ Tsp. Onion powder
* ¼ Tsp. Salt
* ¼ Tsp. Pepper
* 1 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese

### Instructions

Melt butter and add in garlic, onion powder, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Then coat wing with the butter mixture and add Parmesan cheese and serve immediately. This recipe makes enough for about 24 wings.
