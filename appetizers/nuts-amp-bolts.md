### Ingredients

* 1 Box Cheerios
* 1 Box Shreddies
* 1 Bag of Pretzels
* 1 Bag of Cheddar Goldfish
* 1 Bag of Hickory Sticks
* 1 Bag of Bugles
* 1 lb. Mixed Nuts
* 1 lb. Peanuts

Combined all of the above ingredients into a large bread bag and mix.

**Spice Mix:**

* 2 Envelopes of onion soup mix
* 1 1/2 Cups Vegetable Oil
* 2 Tbsp. Celery Salt
* 2 Tbsp. Garlic Salt
* 1 Tbsp. Cayenne pepper

### Instructions

Heat oil in a glass measuring cup in the microwave for about 1-2 minutes. Then add the remaining ingredients and stir so that the dry ingredients dissolve. Pour spice mixture into the bag of mixed snacks, shake and toss the bag so that everything gets an even coating. Pour the mixture into a roaster and bake at 250 for two and a half hours. Stirring every half hour so the bottom mixture doesn't burn. Store in air tight containers.
