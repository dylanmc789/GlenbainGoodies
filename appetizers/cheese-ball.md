# Cheese Ball

By:

### Ingredients

* 1 Pkg. 8 oz. Cream Cheese (Softened)
* 1 Cup Grated Cheddar Cheese
* 2/3 Cup Chopped Ham or Salami
* ½ Cup Chopped Dill Pickles
* 2 tbsp. Mayonnaise
* 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce

### Instructions

Blend until smooth. Cover and chill until firm. Roll ball in ½ cup chopped nuts and decorate with parsley.
