### Ingredients

½ Cup Ketchup  
¼ Cup Water  
¼ Cup Honey  
¼ Cup Red Wine Vinegar  
2 Tbsp. Brown Sugar  
2 Tbsp. Dry Minced Onion  
1 Tbsp. Dijon Mustard  
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce  
1 Tbsp. Soya Sauce  
2 Tbsp. Tabasco  
2 Tbsp. Minced Garlic  

### Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil.  
Coat cooked chicken wings with sauce and put in the oven on broil.  
Watch closely.
