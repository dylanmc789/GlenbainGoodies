### Ingredients

* ½ Cup Soya Sauce
* 1 Tsp. Ginger
* ¼ Cup Water
* 1 Tsp. Onion Powder
* ½ Cup Brown Sugar
* 1 Tsp. Corn Starch
* 1 Tsp. Crushed Garlic

### Instructions

Combine all of the ingredients into a sauce pan and bring to a boil and let thicken add more corn starch if needed. This recipe makes enough for about 24 wings. Also this recipe is a great marinade for Salmon Fillets.
