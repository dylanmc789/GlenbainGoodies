### Ingredients

* ½ Cup Vegetable Oil
* ½ Cup Celery Chopped
* ½ Cup Chopped Onions
* 4 Cups Cooked Rice
* 6 Eggs
* 1 Cup Sliced Chicken or Ham
* 1 Cup Peas

### Instructions

Pour vegetable oil into frying pan and fry chopped celery and onions, scramble eggs and add to celery and onion mixture. Add meat, peas and cooked rice then add a ½ cup of soya sauce add more if needed and it’s ready to eat.
