### Ingredients

* ½ Cup Chicken of Broth
* ¼ Cup Honey
* ¼ Cup Soya Sauce
* 2 Tbsp. Ketchup
* ¼ Tsp. Ginger
* 1 Tsp. Minced garlic

### Instructions

Brown pork chops and place in a baking dish. Mix ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil and pour over browned pork chops. Cover and cook on low heat in a fry pan for 30-40 minutes or put in the oven at 350 F. This recipe makes enough sauce for about 6 pork chops.
