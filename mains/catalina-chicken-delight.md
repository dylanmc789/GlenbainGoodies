### Ingredients

* Chicken (without skin)
* 1 Bottle Catalina Salad Dressing
* 1 envelope onion soup mix
* 1 can cranberry sauce

### Instructions

Place Chicken in roaster without skin. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes. Drain grease.

Add Catalina Salad Dressing, onion soup mix, and cranberry sauce.

Place in roaster and bake 1 Â½ hrs at 350 degrees or until cooked.
