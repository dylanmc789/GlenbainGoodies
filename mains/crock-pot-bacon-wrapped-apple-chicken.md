### Ingredients

* 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
* 1 cup BBQ sauce
* ¼ cup brown sugar
* ⅛ cup lemon juice, fresh or from a bottle
* 1 tbsp. Chili powder
* 4 small apples, peeled and chopped, I used granny smith
* 8 slices bacon

### Instructions

In a small bowl, combine BBQ sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice, and chili powder and peeled/chopped apples.

Wrap each chicken breast with two slices of bacon and place in a greased crock-pot.

Pour BBQ-apple mixture over chicken and cook on low for 6-8 hours or until chicken is done.
