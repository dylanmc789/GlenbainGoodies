### Ingredients

1 ½ lbs. Ground Beef  
1 Egg, beaten  
1 cup Rolled Oats  
1 cup Milk  
1 ½ tsp. Onion soup mix  
½ tsp. Parsley Flakes  
½ tsp. Salt  
1/8 tsp. Pepper  
1 tsp. Worcestershire Sauce  
3 tbsp. Ketchup  

### Instructions

Combine above ingredients.  
Pack lightly into a 9 x 13 pan.  
Make three diagonal indentations across the top and fill with 3 tbsp. Ketchup.  
Bake at 350 F for 1 ¼ to 1 ½ hours.  

**Variations:**  

**Cheddar Meat Loaf:**  
Prepare meat loaf and spread ½ in a loaf pan.  
Cover with 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese.  
Then add remaining meat mixture.  
Bake as directed.  
Leave in pan for about 10 minutes before removing.
