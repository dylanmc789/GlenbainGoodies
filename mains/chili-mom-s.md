### Ingredients

* 1 ½ - 2 lb. Hamburger
* 1 Tbsp. Onion Soup Mix
* 1 Tsp. Salt
* 1 Tsp. Pepper
* 1 Tsp. Minced Garlic
* 2 Tbsp. Chili Powder
* 1 Can Tomatoes (796 ML)
* 1 Can Brown Beans (398 ML)
* 1 Can Kidney Beans (398ML)
* 1 Can Tomato Soup (284ML)

### Instructions

In a large pot brown your meat with Onion soup mix, salt, pepper, garlic, and chili powder. Blend your tomatoes in the blender then pour half of it into the pot, with the other half still in the blender add your kidney beans and pulse them. When done add them to the pot along with the rest of the ingredients. Let simmer on low until your ready to eat.

Optional: You may add other ingredients such as corn, mushrooms or whatever you like.
