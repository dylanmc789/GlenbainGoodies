### Ingredients

* 2 tbsp. Salt
* 2 tbsp. Chilli Powder
* 2 tbsp. Wh. Sugar
* 2 tbsp. Fresh Ground Pepper
* 2 tbsp. Br. Sugar
* 1 tbsp. Cayenne Pepper
* 2 tbsp. Ground Cumin
* ¼ tsp. Paprika

### Instructions

 Mix together, and rub on meat.
