### Ingredients

2 Eggs, beaten  
¾ cup Warm Water  
1 ½ cups Bread Crumbles  
1 pkg. Onion Soup Mix  
1 can Tomato Soup  
2 tbsp. Prepared Mustard  
2 lbs Ground Beef  

### Instructions

Beat eggs in a mixing bowl and add ½ can of tomato soup.  
Add the rest of the ingredients and mix well.  
Butter a loaf pan and pack loaf into pan.  
Pour remaining tomato soup over loaf.  
Bake at 350° F for 1 ½ hours.
