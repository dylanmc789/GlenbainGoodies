### Ingredients

**Meatballs:**  
1 lb Hamburger  
½ cup Quick Rolled Oats  
½ Onion, chopped  
Salt & Pepper to taste  
½ tsp. Real Garlic or ¼ tsp. Garlic Salt  

**Sauce:**  
6 tbsp. Brown Sugar  
½ tsp. Real Garlic  
¼ tsp. Dry Mustard  
2 tbsp. Cornstarch  
½ tsp. Onion Salt or Onion Soup Mix  
1 ¼ cups Water  
¼ cup Vinegar  
6 tbsp. Ketchup  
1 tbsp. Soya Sauce  
Salt & Pepper to taste  

### Instructions

Roll hamburger mixture into walnut-size balls.  
Place on a cookie sheet and bake in oven.  

For sauce, combine all sauce ingredients in a saucepan and let simmer to thicken.  
Pour sauce over baked meatballs.  
Bake in oven for 30 minutes at 350°F.
