### Ingredients

* 1 ½ - 2 lb. Hamburger
* 1 Tsp. Minced Garlic
* 1 Tbsp. Onion Soup Mix
* 2 Cans Tomato sauce (398 ML)
* 1 Tsp. Salt
* 1 Can Tomatoes (796 ML)
* 1 Tsp. Pepper
* 1 Tbsp. Oregano

### Instructions

In a large Pot brown meat with salt, pepper, Garlic, and onion soup mix. If your tomatoes are whole or diced put them in the blender and plus them until liquid. Then add them in with your meat along with your tomato sauce and oregano. Let Simmer on low until your ready to eat.

Optional: you can add about 2 cups of mozzarella cheese and let it melt in the with the meat sauce to give it a little more flavor.
