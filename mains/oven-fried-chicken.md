### Ingredients

* 1 chicken, cut up
* ¼ cup melted butter
* ½ tsp. salt
* ½ tsp. dried mustard
* ½ tsp. paprika

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 375°. Greased pan, place chicken in pan. Combine all sauce ingredients and baste chicken. Bake for 1 hour. No need to turn or baste again. Do not cover.
