### Ingredients

* ¼ Cup Soya Sauce
* ¼ tsp. Paprika
* 2 tbsp. Vegetable Oil
* ¼ tsp. Pepper
* 2 tbsp. Lemon Juice
* ¼ tsp. Ginger
* ¼ tsp. Garlic
* 1 tsp. Montreal Steak Seasoning

### Instructions

Mix the above ingredients in either a glass bowl or plastic bag and add steak. The longer you let the steak marinate the better it well taste!
