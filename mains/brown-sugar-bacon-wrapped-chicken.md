### Ingredients

* 1 Cup packed brown sugar
* 3 Tbps minced/crushed Garlic
* 1 Tbps roasted red pepper seasoning
* 1/2 Tsp Salt
* 1/2 Tsp Pepper
* 6 boneless, chicken breasts or 12 quartered
* 12 Slices of bacon

### Instructions

1. Heat Oven to 375° F
2. In a large bowl, mix brown sugar, garlic, roasted red peppers, salt, and pepper.
3. Coat chicken breasts in the sugar mixture.
4. Wrap each breast with 2 slices bacon, (one if using the quartered chicken) tucking ends under to prevent unwrapping.
5. Place on a baking sheets, Sprinkle any remaining sugar mixture over top.
6. Bake for 25–30 minutes, until bacon is browned and chicken is cooked through. Tip: If bacon isn’t crisp, broil 1–2 minutes—watch closely.
7. Avoid letting the sugar burn; if it darkens too fast, tent loosely with foil.
8. Cook to an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

   **Notes & Serving Ideas**

   Make Ahead: Coat and wrap chicken up to 24 hours ahead; refrigerate, then bake as directed.

   Serve With: Roasted vegetables, salad, rice, or mashed/roasted potatoes to balance the sweet &savory glaze.
