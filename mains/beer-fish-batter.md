### Ingredients

* ¾ cup flour
* 2 beaten eggs
* ¼ cup cornstarch
* ½ bottle beer
* 1 tsp. baking powder
* 1 tsp. nutmeg
* 1 tsp. salt

### Instructions

Mix flour, cornstarch, baking powder and salt. Add eggs, beer and nutmeg. Dip fish in mix until well coated. Fry fish in deep fryer for 7-10 min. or in a skillet over medium heat for about 10 minutes or until golden brown, turning once.
