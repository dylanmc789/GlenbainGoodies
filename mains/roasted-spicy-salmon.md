### Ingredients

* 3-4 lbs. whole salmon
* 1 bunch cilantro chopped
* ¼ cup parsley
* 2 tbsp. chopped mint leaves
* 2 jalapeno peppers (seeds removed )
* 1 tbsp. minced fresh ginger
* 2 garlic cloves chopped
* 2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
* 2 tbsp. oil
* 2 tsp. cumin seeds toasted
* 1 tsp. salt
* ½ cup diced tomato’s

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 425°. Cut diagonal slashes about 4” long 1 ½ “apart and ½ deep on each side of salmon. In food processor put all ingredients in and pulse to make a paste. Stuff mixture into slits and belly cavity of fish. Cover and refrigerate for 1-4 hours. Transfer salmon to a slightly oiled roasting pan. Cook until fish flakes easily along both sides of backbone. Cook for about 40 minutes.
