### Ingredients

**Dry Ingredients:**  
2 Cups Flour  
1 Tbsp. Baking Powder  
1 Tsp. Salt  
½ Tsp. Baking Soda  

**Add:**  
1 Cup Plain Yogurt (or sour cream)  
¼ Cup Butter  
1 Egg  
½ Cup Grated Mozzarella Cheese  

Optional:  
¼ Cup diced pepperoni  
¼ Cup diced peppers  
Pizza Sauce and additional Grated Cheese for layering  

### Instructions

Mix together dry ingredients.  
Make a well in the dry ingredients and add yogurt, butter, egg, and grated cheese.  
Mix until blended.  
In muffin tins (greased or lined), layer as follows:  
- Muffin batter  
- 1 Tbsp. Pizza Sauce  
- Muffin batter  
- 1 Tbsp. Pizza Sauce  
- ½ Tbsp. Grated Cheese  

Bake at 400F for 15-20 minutes.  
Makes 12-16 large muffins.
