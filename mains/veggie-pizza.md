### Ingredients

2 Pkg. Pillsbury Crescent Rolls  
1 Pkg. Cream Cheese  
¾ Cup Mayonnaise  
½ Cup Parmesan Cheese  
1 Tsp. Italian Seasoning  
4 Veggies of your choice  
Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, for topping  

### Instructions

Roll out Pillsbury crescent rolls on a cookie sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes or until brown at 350 F.  
Let cool.  
Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning until well blended.  
Spread mixture on rolls.  
Finely chop the veggies you chose and spread over the cheese layer.  
Scatter a little shredded mozzarella cheese on top.  
Cut and it’s ready to serve.
