### Ingredients

* 10 Slices of Fried Bacon
* 2 Cups Chopped Salami or Pepperoni
* 1 Can of Tomato Soup
* 1 Can of Mushrooms
* 1 Tsp. Oregano
* ¼ Tsp. Salt
* ¼ Tsp. Pepper
* 1 Tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
* 3-4 Drops of Tabasco
* 1-2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese

### Instructions

Combine all ingredients and mix well. Spoon a Tbsp. of mixture on to opened faced buttered buns. Broil buns until melted. (Watch carefully).
