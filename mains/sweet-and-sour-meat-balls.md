### Ingredients

* 1 lb. Hamburger
* 1 Egg
* 1 Cup of crushed Soda Crackers
* ¼ tsp. Salt and Pepper
* 1 tbsp. onion soup mix
* ¼ tsp. Garlic salt or powder

### Instructions

Mix the above ingredients well and roll into small balls, put onto cookie sheet and brown in the oven at 350 F, once browned put into a roaster.

# Sweet and Sour Sauce

#

### Ingredients

* 6 tbsp. Br .Sugar
* ¼ Cup Vinegar
* ½ tsp. Garlic Salt
* 6 tbsp. Ketchup
* ¼ tsp. Dry Mustard
* ½ tsp. Salt
* 2 tbsp. Cornstarch
* ½ tsp. onion Salt (Optional)
* 1 ¼ Cups Water
* 1 tbsp. Soya Sauce

### Instructions

Put the ingredients into a saucepan and bring to a boil remove from heat and pour over meat balls in roaster. Let it sit in the oven at 300 F for a little while (about 30 minutes but the longer you leave it the better the flavor and they will become more tender) to absorb the sweet and sour taste.
