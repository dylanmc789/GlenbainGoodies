### Ingredients

* 1 lb ground beef
* 1 ½ cups water
* 1 large onion chopped
* ½ cup uncooked rice
* 1 cup celery chopped
* ¼ cup soya sauce
* 1 cam cream of mushroom soup
* 1 can bean sprouts
* 1 can cream of celery soup
* 1 can Chinese noodles

### Instructions

Mix together and bake 30 minutes at 350°covered, then bake for 30 minutes more without a lid.  Sprinkle Chinese noodles on top & bake another 15 minutes.
