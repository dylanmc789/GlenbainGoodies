### Ingredients

* 1 Pkg. Hash browns
* ½ cup margarine melted
* 1 small onion chopped
* 1 cup sour cream
* 1 can of celery soup
* ¾ cup Cheez Whiz
* 2 cups crushed Corn Flakes
* 1/2 cup margarine melted

### Instructions

Heat soup, cheese and sour cream until mixed together. Grease 9x13” pan. Place hash browns in bottom of pan; pour ½ cup melted margarine over hash brown. Mix other melted margarine with cornflakes. Put onions over hash brown. Place sour cream mixture over onions. Add Cornflakes mixture to top. Bake for 1 hr at 350 degrees.
