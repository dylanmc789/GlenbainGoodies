### Ingredients

* 1 lb. ground beef
* ½ cup chopped onion
* ¼ cup soya sauce
* 1 cup chopped celery
* 1 can cream of mushroom soup
* 1 can cream of celery soup
* ¼ tsp. pepper
* ½ cup uncooked rice
* 1 can sliced water chestnuts (use water in can)
* 1 cup peas (frozen is best)
* 1 pkg. (4oz.) chow mein noodles

### Instructions

Brown beef and onion. Add rest of ingredients except, chow mein noodles and pour into 2 qt casserole dish. Bake at 350° for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Sprinkle noodles on top and bake uncovered for 15 minutes.
