### Ingredients

* 1 lg. Can Libby Beans
* 2 Tbsp. molasses
* 2 Cans whole Mushrooms (Drained)
* ½ lb. Bacon
* 1 Bottle Heinz Chili Sauce
* 1 Spanish Onion
* ½ Cup Br. Sugar
* 1 Green Pepper
* ½ Tsp. Dry Mustard

### Instructions

Combine beans, mushrooms, and chilli sauce in a baking dish. Mix br. Sugar, dry mustard, and molasses and pour over bean mixture, mix well. Fry bacon, Spanish onion, and green pepper, chopped finely and mix together with the bean mixture and bake at 350F for 1 ½ hours and add 1 can of drained pineapple chunks and the end.
