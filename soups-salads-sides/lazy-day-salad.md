### Ingredients

* 1 Lg. Pkg. Instant Vanilla Pudding
* 2 cans of Mandarin Oranges (drained)
* 1 can of Crushed Pineapple (Not drained)
* 1 (8oz) container of Cool Whip

### Instructions

Mix these ingredients together in a large bowl and then add crushed nuts on top and toasted coconut. (I use 2 cans of crushed pineapple drained or 2 cans of fruit salad cocktail.)
