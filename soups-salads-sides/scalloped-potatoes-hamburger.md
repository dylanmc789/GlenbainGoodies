### Ingredients

1 lb Hamburger  
1 Onion, chopped finely  
Sliced Potatoes  
Dash of Salt and Pepper  
2 Cans Cream of Mushroom Soup  
2 Cups Milk  

### Instructions

Brown hamburger with onion, salt, and pepper.  
Spread browned hamburger on the sliced potatoes in a 2 ½ qt. bowl.  
Heat soup and milk, then pour over potatoes and hamburger.  
Bake at 375° F for 1 ½ hours uncovered.
