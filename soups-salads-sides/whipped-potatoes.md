### Ingredients

Potatoes, cooked and mashed (amount flexible)  
1/3 cup Sour Cream  
½ cup Cream Cheese  
1 cup Cheddar Cheese  
½ tsp Onion Salt  
2 tbsp Butter or Margarine  
Salt and Pepper, to taste  

### Instructions

Whip all ingredients together.  
Cook uncovered at 350°F in the oven for 45 minutes.
