### Ingredients

Salad:

* 2 Cups Cooked Marconi
* 1 Cup Chopped Celery
* 4 Eggs hard boiled (sliced)
* 1 Diced Cucumber
* 3 Finley Chopped Green Onions
* 1 Cup Cheddar Cheese

Dressing:

* ½ Cup Miracle Whip
* ½ Tsp. Dry Mustard
* 1 Tbsp. Prepared Mustard
* ½ Tsp. Seasoning Salt
* ½ Sm. Bottle French Dressing
* ½ Tsp. Garlic Powder
* ½ Tsp. Celery Salt

### Instructions

Toss salad ingredients together in a large bowl and set aside. Whisk the dressing ingredients together and pout over salad and fold until well coated.
