### Ingredients

* 1 cup Caesar salad dressing
* ½ cup sour cream
* 2 tbsp Parmesan cheese
* 1 finely chopped green onion
* Dash of cayenne pepper to taste

### Instructions

Mix together in a glass bowl and store in the fridge until ready to serve with cactus cuts or vegetables.
