### Ingredients

* 2 cans pork and beans
* ¼ cup ketchup
* 1/8 tsp. dry mustard
* 2 tbsp. Br. Sugar
* 1 tbsp. chopped onion
* 4 stripes of cooked bacon or ham

### Instructions

Bake at 350° for 1 hour. If it seems too dry add a little tomato juice.
