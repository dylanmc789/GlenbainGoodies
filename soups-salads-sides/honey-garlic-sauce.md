### Ingredients

* 2 Tbsp. Brown Sugar
* 2 Tbsp. Cornstarch
* ¾ Cup Water
* 3 Tbsp. Soy Sauce
* ¾ Cup Honey
* 2 Tbsp. Garlic

### Instructions

Combine brown sugar with cornstarch in a saucepan, and then add water, soya sauce, honey, and garlic. Bring to a boil stirring constantly. Pour sauce over your cooked meat and serve immediately or keep warm in a low heat oven.
