### Ingredients

* ¾ cup of Olive Oil
* ¾ cup Sugar
* 1 Tsp. Salt
* 1 Tsp. onion powder
* 1 cup strawberries
* 1 Tsp. Dry Mustard
* ⅓ cup Vinegar
* 1 ½ Tbsp. Poppy seeds

### Instructions

Place all ingredients in a blender & Mix well.

Wash Spinach & Pour desired amount of Salad Dressing over spinach. Garnish with Roasted Almonds & Strawberry pieces.
