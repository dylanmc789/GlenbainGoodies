### Ingredients

* 48 oz. Orange Juice
* 48 oz. Pineapple Juice
* ½ cup Strawberries
* 1 pkg. Strawberry Kool-Aid crystals
* 1 cup Sugar
* 1 can Pink Lemonade
* 2 liter Ginger Ale
* 3 cups Water or Ice
* ¾ cup real Lemon Juice

### Instructions

Mix together in large punch bowl. Put ice and ginger ale in last. Makes about 50 cups.
