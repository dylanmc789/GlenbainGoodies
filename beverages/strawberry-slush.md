### Ingredients

* 3 Cups Water
* ¾ Cup Wh. Sugar
* 1 Pkg. Strawberry Kool-aid
* 1 Can Frozen Orange Juice
* 1 Can Frozen Lemonade
* 1 ½ Cup Frozen Strawberries
* 1/3 Cup Mango Juice

### Instructions

Boil water and sugar. Then add remaining ingredients. Freeze in ice cream containers. Scoop the amount of slush you want into a glass and add Ginger ale.
