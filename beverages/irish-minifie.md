### Ingredients

* 1 Cup Powdered Non-Dairy Creamer
* 1 Cup Instant Chocolate Drink Mix
* 2/3 Cup Instant Coffee
* 1/4 Cup White Sugar
* 1/4 Tsp. Nutmeg
* 1/8 Tsp. Cinnamon

### Instructions

Combine above ingredients and store in an air tight container.

Add 2 heaping teaspoons for 1 Cup of boiling water.
