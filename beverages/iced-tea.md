### Ingredients

* 1 Gallon (4 litres) boiling water
* 5 teabags
* 1 can frozen lemonade
* 4 cups cold water
* 2 cups sugar

### Instructions

Steep teabags in boiling water until desired darkness. Let cool and mix remaining ingredients. Refrigerate until cold. Serve with ice and a slice of lemon.
