### Ingredients

* 1 Can of Frozen Orange juice
* 1 Can of Frozen Pink Lemonade
* 1 Carton of Pineapple Juice
* 3 Cups water
* 1 Cup Wh. Sugar
* 1 Pkg. Strawberry Kool-Aid Crystals
* 1 bottle of Ginger ale

### Instructions

Mix all of the ingredients together, add ice and its ready to serve.
