### Ingredients

* 1 Cup Wh. Sugar
* 1 Cup Br. Sugar
* 1 Cup Butter
* ½ Cup Milk
* 2 Eggs
* 2 Tsp. Root Beer Extract
* 2 Tsp. Vanilla Extract
* 4 Cups Flour
* 1 Tsp. Baking Soda
* ¼ Tsp. Salt

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 F. In a large bowl combine sugars, butter, milk, eggs, root beer extract, and vanilla; mix until blended. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Beat until a soft dough forms. Drop dough 1 to 1 ½ Tsp. size balls onto greased cookie sheet. Place the dough balls approximately 2 inches apart. Bake them for 10 minutes or until the cookies are just turning brown. Cool Completely

\*For the Frosting on the cookies use a butter icing and add about a Tbsp. of root beer Extract.\*
