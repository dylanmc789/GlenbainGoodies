### Ingredients

* 1 cup butter
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1 cup Br. sugar
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1 cup Wh. sugar
* 2 cups oatmeal
* 2 eggs
* 1 cup coconut
* 2 tsp vanilla
* 2 cups rice krispies
* 1 ½ cup flour

### Instructions

Cream butter well, add sugars, eggs, and vanilla. Sift flour, baking powder and baking soda together, add to creamed mixture. Stir in oatmeal and coconut. Lastly and rice krispies. Drop onto greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350° for 10-12 minutes.
