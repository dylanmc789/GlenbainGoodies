### Ingredients

* 2 cups sugar
* ½ cup cocoa
* ½ cup milk
* ½ cup marg.
* 2 cups oatmeal
* 1 ½ cups coconut
* 1 tsp. vanilla

### Instructions

Bring sugar, milk and cocoa to a rolling boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat. Add margarine stir until melted and pour over oatmeal, coconut and vanilla. Drop onto wax paper let cool.
