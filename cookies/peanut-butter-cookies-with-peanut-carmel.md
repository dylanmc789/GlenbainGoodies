### Ingredients

**Cookie Dough:**  
1 Cup Butter  
1 Cup Br. Sugar  
1 Cup Wh. Sugar  
3 Large Eggs  
1 Cup Peanut Butter  
2 Tsp. Vanilla  
3 ½ Cups Flour  
1 Tsp. Baking Soda  
½ Tsp. Salt  

**Peanut Caramel Sauce:**  
2 Cups Heavy Cream  
1 Cup Water  
2 Cups Wh. Sugar  
2 Tbsp. Corn Syrup  
¼ Tsp. Coarse Salt  
1 ½ Cup Finely Chopped Peanuts  
1 pkg. Chocolate Chips, melted (optional)  

### Instructions

Cream first 6 ingredients until smooth, then add dry ingredients and mix well.  

**Peanut Caramel Sauce:**  
Combine water, sugar, syrup, and salt in a saucepan and cook into a caramel.  
Remove from heat and whisk in hot cream in a slow, steady stream.  

Make walnut-size balls of cookie dough, place on cookie sheet, and press thumb to make a depression in each one.  
Bake at 350 F for 10-12 minutes.  
Cool, then spoon caramel into each cookie, letting it set for an hour.  
Drizzle with chocolate.
