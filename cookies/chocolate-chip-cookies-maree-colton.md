### Ingredients

* 1 cup Br. sugar
* 2 cups oatmeal
* 1 cup marg.
* ½ tsp salt
* 1 cup flour
* 1 tsp baking soda
* ½ cup chocolate chips

### Instructions

Cream margarine and sugar together, then add remaining ingredients. Add egg last. Bake at 350° for 8-9 minutes.
