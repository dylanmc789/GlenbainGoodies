### Ingredients

* 1 ½ Cup Butter (Opt. ½ Cup Coconut Oil)
* 1 ½ Cup White Sugar
* 1 Cup Br. Sugar
* 2 Tsp. Vanilla
* 4 Eggs
* 3 ½ Cups Flour (add more if needed)
* 1 Cup Cocoa
* 1 ½ Tsp. Baking Soda
* 1 Tsp. Baking Powder
* ½ Tsp. Salt
* 2 Cups White Chocolate Chips
* 1 Cup Chopped nuts (Optional)

### Instructions

Cream butter, eggs, and both sugars until smooth then add dry ingredients. (Opt. you can substitute 1 cup of oat bran and 1 cup ground flax for each cup of flour.) Mix in white chocolate chips and chopped nut if you want them. Bake for 7 minutes at 375F.
