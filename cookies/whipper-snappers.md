### Ingredients

* 1 Egg
* 1 Container of Cool Whip
* 1 Cake mix of your Choice

### Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 F. Mix egg, cool whip, and cake mix coat your hands with icing sugar and roll dough into balls. Place on cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes, makes 36 cookies.
