### Ingredients

* 2 cups butter
* 2 tsp. soda
* 2 packages of 8 oz. Hersheyâ€™s milk choc. Chips
* 1 tsp. salt
* 3 cups flour
* 2 cups Wh. sugar
* 2 cups brown sugar
* 4 cups blended oatmeal
* 6 eggs
* 2 tsp. baking powder
* 2 tsp. vanilla
* 1 cup oat bran

### Instructions

Measure oatmeal, and blend in a blender to a fine powder. Cream the butter and both sugars. Add eggs and vanilla, mix together with flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder, and soda. Add chocolate chips. Roll into balls, and place two inches apart on a cookie sheet. Bake for 10 minutes at 375 degrees. Makes 112 cookies. \*(Optional you can add smarties, skor chunks, and nuts.)
