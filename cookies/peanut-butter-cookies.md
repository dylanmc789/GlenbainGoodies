### Ingredients

* 1 ¼ cup marg.
* 2 tsp. vanilla
* 2 eggs
* 1 cup peanut butter
* 1 cup Br. Sugar
* ¼ tsp. baking soda
* 1 cup Wh. sugar
* ½ tsp. baking powder
* ½ tsp. salt
* 2 ½ cups flour

### Instructions

Beat the eggs, add sugar and margarine and beat till smooth. Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly, then form dough into walnut sized balls. Put on greased cookie sheet and press with fork. Bake at 375° for 10-12 minutes makes 36.
