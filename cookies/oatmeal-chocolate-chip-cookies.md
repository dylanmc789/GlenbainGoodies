### Ingredients

* 1 Cup Butter
* 2 Cups Brown Sugar
* 2 Eggs
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla
* 2 Cups Flour
* 1 Tsp. Baking Powder
* 1/2 Tsp. Baking Soda
* 2 Cups Rolled Oats
* 1 Pkg. Chocolate Chips
* 3/4 Cup Coconut

### Instructions

Cream butter and sugar, beat in eggs and vanilla. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Drop spoonfuls of cookie dough onto ungreased cookie sheet and bake at 350Â° for 10-12 minutes. Flatten with fork if desired.
