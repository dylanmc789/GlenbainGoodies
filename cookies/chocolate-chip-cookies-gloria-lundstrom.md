### Ingredients

* 1 ¼ Cup Flour
* 1 Tsp. Baking Soda
* ¾ Cup Br. Sugar
* 1 Cup Butter
* 1 Tsp. Salt
* ¾ Cup Wh. Sugar
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla
* 2 Eggs
* 1 Pkg. Chocolate Chips

### Instructions

Beat butter and both sugars until light and fluffy. Add eggs beat until airy then add the rest of the ingredients and mix well. Bake at 375 for 10-12 minutes.
