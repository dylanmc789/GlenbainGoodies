### Ingredients

* 1 lb. Butter (2 cups)
* 1/2 cup corn starch
* 1 cup icing sugar
* 3 cups white flour
* I teaspoon vanilla
* \*cherries, hazel nuts, chocolate chips etc.optional to top

### Instructions

Cream butter then add  
1/2 cup corn starch and cream well,  
Add 1 cup icing sugar and cream well,  
Add 3 cups flour, one at a time and cream well,  
Add teaspoon vanilla.  
Spoon or use cookie press onto cookie sheet.  
Bake at 350 for 8 to 12 minutes, lightly browned on bottom.  
Cool on cookie sheet for 10 minutes before moving to rack.  
These cookies freeze well, stack carefully with wax paper.
