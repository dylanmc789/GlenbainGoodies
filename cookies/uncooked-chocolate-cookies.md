### Ingredients

½ cup Butter or Margarine  
½ cup Cocoa  
½ cup Milk  
2 cups Brown Sugar  
2 cups Rolled Oats  
Optional: ½ cup Coconut, cherries, or nuts  

### Instructions

Put butter, sugar, and cocoa into a saucepan.  
Bring to a boil, stirring often.  
Boil for 5 minutes. *(Don’t stir constantly or they will go sugary.)*  
Remove from heat and stir in rolled oats.  
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper.  
Freeze for 5 minutes, then they are ready to serve.  
Makes about 4 dozen.  
Optional: You can add coconut, cherries, or nuts (about ½ cup).
