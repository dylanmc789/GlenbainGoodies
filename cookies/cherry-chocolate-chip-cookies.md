### Ingredients

* ½ cup marg.
* ½ tsp. salt
* ¾ cup Wh. sugar
* 1 cup choc. chips
* 1 egg
* ¾ cup coconuts
* ½ tsp. almond extract
* ¾ cup chopped maraschino cherries
* 1 cup flour
* ½ tsp. baking powder

### Instructions

Cream margarine with sugar. Beat in egg and extract. Combine flour, baking powder and salt. Stir into creamed mixture. Mix in choc.chips, coconuts and cherries. Drop by tsp. onto greased cookie sheets. Bake at 350°f for 10-12 minutes. Makes about 3 ½ Dozen cookies.
