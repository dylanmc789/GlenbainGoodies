### Ingredients

* 1 Cup Wh. Sugar
* 1 Cup Br. Sugar
* 1 Cup Softened Butter
* 2 Eggs
* 2 tsp. Vanilla
* 2 ½ Cups Flour
* ¾ Cup Cocoa
* 1 tsp. Baking Soda
* 6 Pkgs. Rolo Chocolates

### Instructions

Cream first five ingredients together. Add everything else (Except rolos) and beat. Take 1 tbsp. of dough, wrap around a rolo, roll and then roll again in sugar. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 F for 8 minutes and leave on cookie sheet for another 2-4 minutes.
