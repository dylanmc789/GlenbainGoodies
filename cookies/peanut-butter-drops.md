### Ingredients

½ cup Butter or Margarine  
½ cup Cocoa  
½ cup Milk  
3 cups Rolled Oats  
1 ¾ cups Brown Sugar  
½ cup Peanut Butter  

### Instructions

Measure butter, milk, brown sugar, and cocoa into a medium saucepan.  
Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring often.  
Boil for 3 minutes. *(Don’t stir constantly or they will go sugary.)*  
Remove from heat and add rolled oats and peanut butter. Mix well.  
Drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper.  
Freeze for 5 minutes, then they are ready to serve.  
Makes about 5 dozen.
