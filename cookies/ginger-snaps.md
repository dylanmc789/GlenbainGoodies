### Ingredients

* 1-¾ cup Crisco (Butter Flavoured)
* 1 cup white sugar
* 1 egg
* ¼ cup molasses
* 2 cups flour
* 2 tsp baking soda
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1 ½ tsp ginger
* ¼ tsp cloves
* ½ tsp salt

### Instructions

Mix Crisco & sugar. Add egg and molasses. Add dry ingredients. Form balls and roll in sugar.  Bake @ 375 Degrees for 7-8 minutes.
