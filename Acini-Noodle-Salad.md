## Ingredients
- 2 cups of cooked acini noodle
- 2 lg can of crushed pineapple (not drained)
- 1 lg  pkg instant vanilla pudding
- 2 cans of mandarin oranges (drained)
- 1 (8oz) container of cool whip

## Instructions
1. Mix these ingredients together in a large bowl and then add toasted almond slices and coconut.
