### Ingredients

* 4 Cups Pickling Vinegar
* 8 Cups Water
* ¾ Cup Coarse Pickling Salt
* 6 Tbsp. Br. Sugar
* ¼ Tsp. Pickling Spice
* Garlic Cloves

### Instructions

Bring 1st 3 ingredients to a rolling boil.  Place Brown Sugar, pickling Spice & Garlic cloves in sterilised jars. Wash cucumbers in cold water pack into the sterilised jars with the spices. Pour boiling brine over cucumbers & seal jars. When jars are sealed place in a canner in a cold water bath & process until cucumbers turn a light shade of green.

NOTE: Do not boil or they’ll turn mushy.

Makes 6 Quarts
