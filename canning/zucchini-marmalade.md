### Ingredients

* 6 cups shredded zucchini
* 6 cups sugar
* 1-19 oz crushed pineapple canned fruit
* 1 ½ oranges
* ½ lemon (blended or pureed)
* 1-6 oz box apricot powdered jello

### Instructions

Boil 1st 5 ingredients slowly for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in jello until dissolved. Pour into sterilized jars and seal with wax.
