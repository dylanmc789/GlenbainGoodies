### Ingredients

* 4 cups cut rhubarb
* 4 cups strawberries
* 2 ½ cups sugar
* 2½ cups water

### Instructions

Boil sugar and water for a short time, and then add fruit. Boil until it thickens like a sauce 2-3 minutes.

Seal in jars.
