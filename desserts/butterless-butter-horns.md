### Ingredients (Dough)

* 3 Cups Flour
* 2 Eggs
* 1 Tsp. Salt
* 1 Tbsp. White Sugar
* 1 Cup Lard
* 1 Cup Warm Milk
* 1 Pkg. Yeast (1 Tbsp.)

### Instructions (Dough)

Mix flour, salt, lard, sugar, and yeast until course like pie dough. Mix eggs and milk to the flour mixture and let chill overnight. In the morning cut dough in 3 sections and roll about 1/8” thick. Cut each circle in 8 wedge shaped pieces and roll up from the large end. Place horns on a greased pans and let rise for 2 hours. Bake at 400F for 10 minutes Ice while hot with a drizzle of thin icing.

### Instructions (Icing)

Use a Tbsp. of cream and 2 Tbsp. of Brown sugar mix together until sugar is dissolved, and it’s ready to use.
