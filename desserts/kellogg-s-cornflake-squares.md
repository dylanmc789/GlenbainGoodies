### Ingredients

* 1/2 Cup Butter
* 1 (400g)  Bag Marshmallows
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla
* 1/2 Cup Slivered Almonds
* 1/2 Cup Shredded Coconut
* 7-8 Cups Cornflakes
* 1 Cup Chocolate Chips (Optional)

### Instructions

In a large microwavable bowl, microwave your butter for 1 minute or until melted. Add marshmallows, toss so the butter covers them. Microwave for 2 minutes, stir, it shouldn't have any whole marshmallows, if need be microwave for another minute so all the marshmallows are melted. Stir in vanilla with a large spatula and add almonds, and coconut. Then add your cornflakes 2 cups at a time until you see that all the cornflakes are evenly coated. Press mixture into a buttered 9 X 13 and place in the fridge to set, about 1 hour. Cut into squares and serve. \*(You can add chocolate chips in with the cornflakes if you like). \*
