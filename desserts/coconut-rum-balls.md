### Ingredients

* 3 cups Vanilla Wafers Crumbs
* 1 ⅓ cups Coconut
* 1 cup finely chopped nuts
* 1 can Eagle Brand Milk
* ¼ cup Rum
* Additional flaked coconut chopped

### Instructions

In large bowl, combine crumbs, coconut and nuts. Add eagle brand and rum mix well, chill 4 hours. Shape into 1” balls. Roll in additional coconut. Store tightly covered in refrigerator. Makes about 8 dozen candies. Flavor of the candies improve after 24 hours. Keep for several weeks.
