### Ingredients

*Base*:

* 2 Cups Crushed Ritz Crackers
* ½ Cup Melted Butter or Marg.

Spread evenly in the bottom of a 9”x 13” pan & bake at 350° for 20 min.

Filling:

* 2 pkgs. Pistachio Instant Pudding Mixes
* 1½ Cups Milk

### Instructions

Mix Pudding & Milk until thick & set.

Add 1 Litre Vanilla Ice Cream softened & 1 container of Cool Whip to filling & mix well.  Pour over base & top with crushed pistachio or other favourite nuts. *Refrigerate before serving.*
