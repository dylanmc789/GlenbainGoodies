### Ingredients

½ Cup Wh. Sugar  
2 tbsp. Cocoa  
2 tbsp. Butter  
¼ Cup Milk  
1 tbsp. Syrup  
Dash Salt  
½ - ¾ Cup Icing Sugar  
1 tsp. Vanilla  

### Instructions

Mix sugar and cocoa in heavy saucepan.  
Blend in butter, milk, syrup, and salt.  
Bring to a boil, stirring frequently.  
Boil vigorously, stirring constantly, for 3 minutes, then let cool.  
Add icing sugar and vanilla, blend until creamy.
