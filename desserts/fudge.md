### Ingredients

* 1 Cup Br. Sugar
* ½ Cup Butter
* 1 Cup White Sugar
* 3 Tbsp. Cocoa
* 1 Cup Cream or 1 Can of evaporated Milk
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla

### Instructions

Boil all of the ingredients for 15 minutes and add ½ Cup flour and ½ Cup chopped nuts. Pour into buttered pans. (Stir as little as possible otherwise it will go sugary).
