### Ingredients

* 1 ½ cups flour
* ¼ cup cocoa
* 1 ½ cup Wh. sugar
* 3 tsp. baking powder
* 1 tsp. salt
* ½ cup oil
* 7 unbeaten egg yolks
* ¾ cup cold water
* 2 tsp. vanilla
* 7 egg whites
* ½ tsp cream of tartar

### Instructions

Beat egg whites and cream of tartar together. Mix wet ingredients well then add dry ingredients put in pan. Bake for 55 minutes at 325°then at 350° for 10-15 minutes. Use a big pan.
