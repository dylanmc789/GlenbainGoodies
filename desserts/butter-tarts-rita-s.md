### Ingredients

* ¾ Cup Corn Syrup
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla
* ¾ Cup Brown Sugar
* 2 Eggs (beaten)
* ¼ Cup Butter
* 1 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
* 1 Cup Currants
* Nut Optional

### Instructions

Cream butter and sugar together mix well. Add syrup, eggs, lemon juice, and vanilla. Add 1 cup currants, carrots or raisins. Mix together, put into uncooked tart shells and bake at 375F for 10-12 minutes.
