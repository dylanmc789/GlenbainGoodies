### Ingredients

½ cup margarine  
1 ½ cups Wh. Sugar  
½ cup cocoa  
1 cup flour  
4 eggs  
2 cups chopped nuts  
1 tsp. vanilla  

**Icing:**  
4 tbsp. margarine or ½ cup  
Dash salt  
1 ½ -2 cups icing sugar  
2 egg whites (unbeaten)  
¼ cup – ½ cup cocoa  

### Instructions

Cream butter, sugar, add cocoa and mix well.  
Beat eggs slightly and mix in.  
Sift flour twice then add nuts and vanilla.  
Pour into a 9 x 13 pan and bake at 350F for 20-25 minutes.
