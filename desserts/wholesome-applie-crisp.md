### Ingredients

8 Medium Size Apples  
½ Cups Raisins (Optional)  
¾ Cup Rolled Oats  
½ Cup Flour  
2/3 Cup Brown Sugar  
3 tbsp. Flour  
¼ tsp. Cinnamon  
¼ tsp. Cinnamon  
½ Cup Cold Butter (cut into bits)  

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°F.  
Place cut-up apples in an 8-inch baking dish and add raisins.  

Blend 1/3 cup brown sugar with 3 tbsp. flour and ¼ tsp. cinnamon.  

In a large bowl, blend remaining 1/3 cup sugar, oats, flour, and ¼ tsp. cinnamon.  
Cut in butter with 2 knives.  
Spread evenly over the fruit and press down gently with the palm of your hand.  
Bake uncovered in preheated oven for 35-45 minutes or until golden brown.  
Serve warm.
