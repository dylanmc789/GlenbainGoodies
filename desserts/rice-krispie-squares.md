### Ingredients

* Â¼ cup Butter
* 1 (400g) small bag Marshmallows
* 1 tsp. Vanilla
* 7 cups Rice Krispies

### Instructions

Melt butter in microwave for 30 seconds. Add marshmallows cook on high for 2 minutes stir. Add vanilla and rice Krispies. Press into greased 9 x 13 pan and let cool before serving.
