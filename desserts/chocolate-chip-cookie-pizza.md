### Ingredients

* 1 ½ Cups Flour
* ½ Cup Melted Butter
* ½ Tsp. Baking Powder
* 1 Cup Brown Sugar
* ½ Tsp. Baking Soda
* 1 Egg
* ½ Tsp. Salt
* 1 Tbsp. Hot Water

### Toppings

* ½ Cup Chocolate Chips
* ¼ Cup M&Ms
* ½ Cup Butterscotch Chips
* 1 Cup Mini Marshmallows

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 F. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix in melted butter and brown sugar. Beat egg and water and add to the flour mixture. Mix well and press into a 12” greased foil pizza pan. Sprinkle topping ingredients over the crust and bake for 20 minutes.
