### Ingredients

* 4 oz Pkg. Milk choc chips
* ½ cup flour
* ¼ cup butter
* ½ cup chopped nuts
* ¾ cup sugar
* 2 ½ cups cold milk
* 2 eggs
* 2 pkg. choc inst pudding
* 1 tsp vanilla
* 1 container of cool whip

### Instructions

Melt choc and butter in microwave. Stir In sugar, eggs and vanilla. Add flour and nuts. Spread in a 9” pie pan or 9 x 13” dish & bake @ 350° for 25-30 min. Let cool.

Beat milk and instant pudding with a whisk for 1 minute. Let stand for 2 minutes. Then spread on brownie pie, top with cool whip. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
