### Ingredients

* 4 Â½ cups Flour
* 2 tbsp. Br. Sugar
* 2 tsp. Baking Powder
* 2 tsp. Salt
* 1 lb. Shortening (Tender-flake)
* 2 tsp. Vinegar
* 1 egg slightly beaten

### Instructions

In large bowl mix 1st 4 ingredients. Cut in shortening until course. Put vinegar, egg, and enough water to make 1 cup and pour over 1st mixture. Mix well. Knead dough and let sit for 5 minutes. Then roll out. Makes about 4 or 5 double pies.
