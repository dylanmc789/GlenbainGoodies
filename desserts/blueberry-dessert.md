### Ingredients

* 2 Cups Graham Wafer Crumbs
* ½ Cup Butter
* 1 Can Blueberry Pie Filling
* 1 Pkg. Instant Vanilla Pudding
* 1 Pkg. Dream Whip
* 2 Cup Fresh Blueberries (Optional)

### Instructions

Melt Butter and mix in graham wafer crumbs, saving some for the topping. Press into “9x13” pan. Pour blueberry filling over crumbs (Optional pour 2 cups of fresh blueberries as well). Mix pudding as directed and pour over blueberries. Mix dream whip and pour over pudding, sprinkle with crumbs and cut into squares. (Optional I fold pudding into whip cream and spread over blueberries).
