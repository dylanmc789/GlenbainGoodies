### Ingredients

1 ½ cup Butter  
3 eggs  
1 ½ cups Wh. Sugar  
¾ cup Br. Sugar  
1 ½ tsp. Baking Soda  
½ tsp. Salt  
1 ½ tsp. Vanilla  
1 large bag of choc. chips  
3 cups flour  

### Instructions

Scoop about a tsp. of cookie dough and roll into balls and place onto cookie sheet. *(Do not grease the cookie sheet)*  
Bake at 350 F for about 8 minutes.  
Makes about 3 dozen cookies.
