### Ingredients

* 1 Cup Chopped Nuts
* 1 ½ Cup Rolled Oats
* 1 Cup Chocolate Chips
* 1 Cup Peanut Butter
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla
* ¾ Cups Honey

### Instructions

Double this recipe for it to fit on a cookie sheet. Melt peanut butter, honey, and chocolate chips. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Spread on ungreased cookie sheet. Let cool and cut. This recipe freezes well.
