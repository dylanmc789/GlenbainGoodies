### Ingredients

* 1 pkg. yeast
* ½ cup warm water
* 1 tbsp. sugar

  (Put above ingredients into a bowl. Let rise for 10 minutes)
* ½ cup margarine
* 1 tsp. salt
* 1 cup scalded milk
* 5 cups flour
* ½ cup sugar
* 3 beaten eggs

### Instructions

Heat milk, sugar, salt and margarine. Cool while yeast mixture is rising. In a larger bowl place, the 5 cups of flour. Once yeast is ready & milk mixture is cool mix both together along with the eggs. Pour liquid mixture over the flour and mix & knead well to get a smooth texture. Let rise do not punch down. Cut off a piece of dough and roll out the size of a small pie plate. Cut into 8-10 wedges, roll wedge from outside in. Place on greased cookie sheet. Let rise to double in size.

Bake at 375° for 12 minutes. Ice and sprinkle with nuts, candy etc.
