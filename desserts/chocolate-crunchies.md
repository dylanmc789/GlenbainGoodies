### Ingredients

* 1 Pkg. Butterscotch Chips
* 1 Pkg. Peanut Butter Chips
* 1 Pkg. Chocolate Chips
* 1 Bag (100g) Bag of Crushed Ripple Chips

### Instructions

Melt ingredients and add 1 (100g) bag of crushed ripple chips place in chocolate molds on little cups.
