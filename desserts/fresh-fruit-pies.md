### Ingredients

* 3 cups Fruit
* 1 tbsp. Butter
* ½ cup sugar or less
* sprinkle of Salt
* 2 tbsp. tapioca
* ½ tsp. Cinnamon

### Instructions

Mix together in a pot on the stove and cook on low until tapioca is soft and fruit mixture has thickened.
