### Ingredients

* 1 pkg. coloured marshmallows
* 4 oz. choc. Chips
* 1 cup icing sugar
* ¼ cup melted butter
* 2 eggs beaten
* ½ cup chopped nuts
* ½ cup chopped cherries
* 1 tsp. vanilla
* Pinch of salt
* ½ cup coconut

### Instructions

Melt choc. and butter then add remaining ingredients.  Roll in ground nuts and flaked coconut. Wrap in wax paper and chill in fridge till your able to cut it.
