### Ingredients

2 boxes (170 g) Jello or 4 boxes (85 g) Jello powder  
2 ½ cups boiling water  

### Instructions

Completely dissolve Jello powder in water.  
Pour into 9 x 13” or cookie sheet pan.  
Chill for 3 hours or until firm.  
Cut into squares and serve.
