### Ingredients

* 2 cups choc. Chips
* 4 cups mini marshmallow’s
* 2 tbsp. shortening
* 1 cup butterscotch chips
* 3 tbsp. Margarine
* 3 quarts cooked popcorn

### Instructions

Melt chips and shortening and set aside. In 9×13 pan greased place popcorn. In a heavy pot melt until smooth margarine, butterscotch chips and marshmallows. Coat popcorn with this mixture.  Place ½ of mixture into 9×13” pan. Then layer with chocolate chips mixture. Spread remaining popcorn mixture over-top. Chill 30 min. cut into squares.
