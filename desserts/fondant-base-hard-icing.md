### Ingredients

* 2 cups white sugar
* 4 egg whites (room temp.)
* ½ cup water
* 1 ½ cup icing sugar
* ¼ tsp cream of tartar (sifted if lumpy)

### Instructions

Boil sugar, water and cream of tarter to 240°f (soft ball stage). Meanwhile, beat egg whites for 7 minutes at high speed. Add boiled sugar to egg whites slowly while mixing. Add icing sugar and beat another 3-5 minutes. Cover with damp cloth while using, **re-beating** will not restore original texture. Keep covered.
