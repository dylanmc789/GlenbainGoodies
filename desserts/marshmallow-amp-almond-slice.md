### Ingredients

* 40 Reg. Marshmallows Or 3 Cups Mini Marshmallows
* 2 ½ Cups Corn Flakes
* 1 Cup Coconut
* ½ Cup Butter
* 1 Cup Slivered Almonds

### Instructions

Melt Marshmallows and butter and add the corn flakes, coconut, and slivered almonds. Then Press into a 9 X 13” pan.
