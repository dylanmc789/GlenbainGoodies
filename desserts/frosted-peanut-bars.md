### Ingredients

* ½ Cup Br. Sugar
* 1 Cup Chopped Peanuts
* ½ Cup Peanut Butter
* 4 Cups Corn Flakes
* ½ Cup Corn Syrup
* 1 Cup Chocolate Chips

### Instructions

Combine first 3 ingredients in a large saucepan. Cook over medium heat till it bubbles. Remove from heat stir in peanuts and corn flakes. Press into a greased 9 X 13” pan and spread melted chocolate on top. Cut into bars when firm.
