### Ingredients

¾ cup sugar (Br or WH)  
2 ½ tbsp cornstarch  
4 tbsp cocoa  
Dash salt  
1 tbsp margarine  
1 egg beaten  
2 cups cold milk  

**Vanilla / Butterscotch Variations:**  

**Vanilla:**  
2 cups cold milk  
Dash salt  
1 egg beaten  
½ cup white sugar  
3 ½ tbsp cornstarch  
1 tbsp margarine  

**Butterscotch:**  
2 cups cold milk  
Dash salt  
1 egg beaten  
¾ cup brown sugar  
2 ½ tbsp cornstarch  
3 tbsp margarine  

### Instructions

Combine first 4 ingredients. Make a paste by adding the egg.  
Add milk 1 cup at a time, add vanilla and margarine, and heat slowly, stirring often.
