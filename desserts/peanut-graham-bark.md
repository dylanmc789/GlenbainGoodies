### Ingredients

1 lb. Vanilla Dipping Chocolate  
1 Cup Salted Peanuts  
4 Cups Golden Graham Cereal  

### Instructions

Melt chocolate on low heat in a large saucepan, then add peanuts and cereal, mixing well.  
Spread onto a large cookie sheet lined with wax paper.  
Cool and cut into bite-size pieces.
