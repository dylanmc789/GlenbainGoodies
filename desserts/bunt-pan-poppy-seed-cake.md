By Mom

### Ingredients

* 1 White or Butter pecan cake mix
* 1/2 Cup Oil
* 1 Tbsp. Rum extract
* 1 Pkg. Vanilla pudding mix
* 4 Eggs
* 1/2 Cup Poppy Seeds
* 1 Cup Milk

### Instructions

Mix together all ingredients. Pour into a greased bunt pan and bake at 350 F for 45 minutes or until you can poke it with a toothpick and it comes out clean.

# Icing

### Ingredients

* 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
* 1/4 Cup Butter
* 1 Tbsp. Rum Extract
* 3 Tbsp. Cream or milk
* 1 1/2 Cups Icing Sugar

### Instructions

In a medium saucepan boil the brown sugar, cream, and butter for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and cool. Add icing sugar and rum. Beat until smooth. If needed add more cream or icing sugar.
