### Ingredients

* 1 cups butter softened
* 1 can eagle brand milk
* ½ sifted icing sugar
* ½ cup butter
* 1 ¼ cup flour
* 1 tsp. vanilla
* 3 tbsp. corn syrup
* ½ pkg chocolate chips
* ¼ tsp. salt

### Instructions

Preheat oven at 350°. In large bowl, beat 1 cup butter, sugar and salt until fluffy. Add flour, mix well. Press evenly into greased 9” square pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until lightly browned, cool slightly.   In large measuring dish, melt ½ cup of butter on high for 1 minute in microwave. Stir in eagle brand and syrup, microwave on high for 6-8 minutes. Stirring after each minute or until mixture turns a light caramel colour. Stir in vanilla. Spread over warm shortbread. Drizzle with chocolate. Chill until firm. Cut into bars store at room temperature.
