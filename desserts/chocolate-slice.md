### Ingredients

* ½ Cup Melted Butter
* 2 Tbsp. Cocoa
* ½ Cup Brown Sugar
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla
* 1 Cup Flour
* 1 Egg

### Instructions

Mix together and place in a greased 8 X 8” pan. 2nd Layer mix ½ can of eagle brand milk and 2 cups of coconut and spread over chocolate base. Bake at 350 F for 20-25 minutes. Then ice with this Chocolate icing or cover with chocolate chips.
