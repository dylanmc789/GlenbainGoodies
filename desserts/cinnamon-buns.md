### Ingredients

The Dough for these cinnamon buns is the same as the bun recipe.  
1/3 cup Br. Sugar  
2 tbsp. Butter  
1 tbsp. Light Corn Syrup  

**Sprinkle Mixture:**  
½ cup Br. Sugar  
1 tsp. Cinnamon  
¼ cup chopped pecans  

### Instructions

Bring the dough mixture to a boil and pour into greased pan.  
Sprinkle each pan with ¼ cup chopped pecans.  
Sprinkle mixture on top of buns and bake at 365 F for 20 minutes.
