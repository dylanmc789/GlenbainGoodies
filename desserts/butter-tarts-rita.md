### Ingredients

* 1 Cup Butter
* 1 Cup Brown Sugar
* 2 Eggs Beaten slightly
* 2 Tsp Vanilla
* ½ Tsp Nutmeg
* ½ Tsp. Salt
* 2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
* 1 Cup Corn Syrup
* 1 Cup Raisins OR Currants
* 1 Cup Crushed Walnuts
* OR Crushed Pecans

### Instructions

Melt Butter, add Sugar, salt, & Corn syrup, slightly beaten eggs, mix until frothy then add remaining ingredients.

Spoon mixture into unbaked tart shells, fill ½ full.

Bake @ 400F for 8-10 min. Then bake @ 375F for 5 more min or until golden brown.
