### Ingredients

* 1 Cup Corn Syrup
* 8 Cups Crispix Cereal
* 1 Cup Brown Sugar
* 1 Cup Smarties
* ½ Cup Peanut Butter
* 1 Cup Peanuts
* ½ Cup Butter
* 1 Tsp. Vanilla

### Instructions

Combine corn syrup, brown sugar, butter, and peanut butter in a saucepan, bring to a boil and add vanilla. Then combine cereal, smarties, and peanuts in a large bowl and add the sauce mixture and mix until all cereal is coated and press into a greased 9 X 13” pan.
