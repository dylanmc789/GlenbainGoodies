### Ingredients

* 1 separated egg
* 1/8 tsp salt
* 4 tbsp sugar
* 2 ¾ cup milk
* 3 tbsp tapioca
* 1 tsp vanilla

### Instructions

Separate 1 egg, beat egg whites until foamy. Add 2 tbsp sugar gradually and beat to soft peaks.

Cook and stir 2 tbsp sugar, the egg yolk, 3 tbsp tapioca, 1/8 tsp salt and 2 ¾ cups milk to full boil. Very slowly add to beaten whites. Stirring rapidly to blend add vanilla. Stir after 20 minutes. Makes 6 servings ½ cup each.
