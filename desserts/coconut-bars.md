### Ingredients

* ½ cup butter
* ½ cup margarine
* 2 tbsp corn syrup
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 2 tsp vanilla
* 3 ½ cups oatmeal
* ½ tsp baking powder
* ½ tsp salt
* 2/3 cup shredded coconut

### Instructions

In a large saucepan melt butter, margarine, corn syrup, and brown sugar. Add vanilla, oatmeal, baking powder, salt and coconut. Mix well and pat onto grease cookie sheet. A fork dipped in cold water helps to spread mixture evenly. Mixture should be about ¼” thick. Bake at 325°f for 15 to 20 minutes. Cut into squares while warm.
