### Ingredients

* ½ Cup Butter
* 2 Eggs
* 1 Cup Brown Sugar
* ¼ Tsp. Salt
* ½ Cup Corn Syrup
* 2 Tsp. Vinegar
* ½ Tsp. Maple Flavoring
* 2/3 Cup Chopped Walnuts

### Instructions

Combine ingredients. You can add raisins if you want. Bake in the oven at 350 for 10 minutes. If you double this recipe it will make 30 tarts.
