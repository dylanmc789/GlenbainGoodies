### Ingredients

* 2 Dozen Frozen Bun Rolls
* 1 Cup Br. Sugar
* ¼ Cup Birds Custard Powder
* 2 Tsp. Cinnamon

### Instructions

Arrange in a greased Bundt pan. Mix Br. Sugar, birds’ custard powder, and cinnamon. Pour on top of rolls and drizzle with ½ Cup melted butter and cover with a wet tea towel and let sit on cupboard overnight and bake at 350F for 25 minutes.
