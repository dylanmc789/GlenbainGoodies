### Ingredients

2 1/3 Cup Sweetened Milk  
¼ Cup Peanut Butter  
¼ Cup Light Corn Syrup  
½ Cup Packed Brown Sugar  
4 Cup Rice Krispie Cereal  

**Icing:**  
½ Cup Chocolate Chips  
2 Tbsp. Peanut Butter  

### Instructions

Cook first four ingredients in a saucepan until well mixed and thickened, stirring constantly.  
Remove from heat and add rice cereal, stirring to coat.  
Let cool and cut into squares.  

**Icing:**  
Melt over low heat and spread over bars.
