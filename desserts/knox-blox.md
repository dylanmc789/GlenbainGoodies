### Ingredients

2 envelopes of Knox Unflavoured gelatin  
3 boxes (3 oz / 85 g each) Jello flavoured gelatin  
4 cups boiling water  

### Instructions

In large bowl, combine Knox gelatin and Jello, and add boiling water.  
Stir until gelatin is dissolved.  
Pour into large shallow baking pan (9 x 13‚Äù).  
Chill until set.  
Cut into squares or use cookie cutters and serve.
