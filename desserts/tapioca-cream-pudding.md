### Ingredients

Combine in Saucepan:

* 3 Tbsp Minute Tapioca
* 1 Egg yolk beaten
* 2 Tbsp Sugar
* 2 cups Milk
* Pinch of Salt

### Instructions

Cook over medium heat stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a full boil. Remove from heat.

Beat until stiff:

* 1 egg white

Add in:

* 2 Tbsp Sugar
* ½ Tsp Vanilla

Gradually fold tapioca mixture into meringue

### **Note:**

To make a chocolate version, add 2 ½ Tbsp. instant chocolate drink powder to milk in above recipe.
