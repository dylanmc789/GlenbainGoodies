### Ingredients

* 2 cups Pumpkin
* ½ tsp. Salt
* 1 cup Sugar
* 3 Eggs
* ½ tsp. Ginger
* ½ cups evaporated milk
* 1 tsp. Cinnamon

### Instructions

Pour above ingredients into unbaked pie shell and bake at 400 F for 15 minutes then 350 F for 30 minutes.
